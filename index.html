Introducing MandarinMate

Your Smart Mandarin Learning Companion

Learning Mandarin characters, pronunciation, and vocabulary can be overwhelming without a structured and interactive approach.

MandarinMate is a Flutter mobile application that helps students master Mandarin through interactive lessons, smart flashcards, a supportive community forum, and an AI tutor.

Download App Learn More

Key Features

Everything you need to master Mandarin in one app.

Interactive Lessons

Learn grammar and vocabulary step-by-step with engaging content.

Smart Flashcards

Memorize characters, pinyin, and meanings efficiently using spaced repetition.

AI Tutor

Practice your pronunciation and get instant feedback with our advanced speech recognition.

Community Forum

Connect with other learners and native speakers to share tips and ask questions.

Learning Dashboard

Track your progress and stay motivated with detailed analytics.
